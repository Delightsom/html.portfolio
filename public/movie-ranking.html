The Best Movies according to Bibi

My Top 3 movies of all time

Amélie

This whimsical French film follows Amélie, a shy waitress in Paris, who decides to change the lives of those around her for the better while grappling with her own isolation and longing for connection.e

The Perks of Being a Wallflower

This coming-of-age drama centers on Charlie, a socially awkward high school freshman, who navigates the complexities of friendship, love, and mental health while learning to embrace his identity and experiences.

Lost in Translation
